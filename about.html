About Me

Hi, I'm Ismael Barron, but most people call me Ish. I'm a middle child of three brothers and I’m very proud with the people I’m surrounded by, they mean everything to me. I’m a student, a part-time worker, and someone who loves to explore my hobbies and passions.

My Life & Work

I currently run a small construction company with my dad and also work part-time at Hertz in the Denver Airport. I've always leaned toward hands on jobs, here are some of the things I've done:

  • Shoveled snow and drove plow trucks in the winter
  • Detailed new apartments in Denver (painting, fixing dishwashers, cabinets, and doors)
  • Worked at UPS as a loader and unloader
  • Concrete finishing and laying
All these jobs have shown me how important it is to work hard, stay flexible, and figure things out as you go. I want to bring that same attitude into my education—taking what I’ve learned from real-life experiences and using it to help me grow, learn new things, and hopefully make a difference.
